The Dictionary of Mormon Biography is a new historical project conceived by Tod Robbins as a modern continuation of Andrew Jenson's Latter-day Saint Biographical Encyclopedia (LDSBE). In it you will find concise, informative descriptions of the lives of representative persons in Latter-day Saint history.

Beginning in June 2012, the first phase of the project includes the conversion of individual biographies from scanned copies of the LDSBE, totaling over 5,000 biographies. I am currently developing a good workflow for this phase. More plans will be shared soon!

Feel free to contact me if you are interested in helping with the project.
