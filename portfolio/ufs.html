Working with the founder of Utah Forest Stewards, I set up and developed the WordPress site for their organization. I administer the site and contribute content occasionally. The UFS site is one of many WordPress sites I have created, designed, or administered in the past 5 years.

I also helped coordinate community outreach as the organization launched. This experience and others have helped me hone my awareness and concentrated use of social media and collaborative tools to make a difference in the communities I have lived in. A good librarian should always be anxiously engaged in a good cause.
