Currently, I am working within the online genealogical community. I am involved in multiple working groups dealing with emerging open data standards for genealogical and historical data schemas and APIs. I am an active member of the Open Genealogical Alliance, a project of the Open Rights Group and the Open Knowledge Foundation, and currently researching the influence of for-profit corporations upon the access rights of genealogical records. We are currently in the beginning stages of developing an open web platform/database of gravestones and other burial markers.

These projects help me to stay abreast of the changing world of researchers, especially genealogists, on the web. I am also a skilled genealogist, especially with researching the western United States and British Isles. I am very familiar with the FamilySearch, Ancestry, and Geni services.
