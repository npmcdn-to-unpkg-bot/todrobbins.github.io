I began the Saints of Alberta Project with a couple of researchers in 2010 seeking to make more cultural resources and historical documents available concerning Latter-day Saints in Alberta, Canada. We have been amassing digital copies of primary sources, including: letters, journals, photographs, etc., to our online archive for the past year and a half. I maintain the website and am in the midst of developing a catalog system for search and retrieval of the documents we have collected.

As the Saints of Alberta Project has been a purely volunteer effort, the effort progressed in an intriguing fashion. The onus on me to maintain professional standards has been immense and yet I am proud to gather a community around a topic and seek to build bridges for sharing culture, history, and information.
