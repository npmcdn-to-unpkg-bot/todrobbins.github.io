I am a web developer on multiple projects with the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration as a graduate student intern. I am building two web applications that serve researchers from various disciplines. One of the applications is for studying and visualizing data extracted from Danish Meteorological Annuals, dating 1893-1908, which may serve as a prototype for other data explorer apps. The other application is being built as an encyclopedic companion site to the Old Weather project by Zooniverse, dealing with United States Coast Guard ship logs.

Users will be able to search the records and media related to ships, people, and places on the the Old Weather service. I have implemented a combination of jQuery libraries, CSS3 animations/transitions, and SIMILE widgets for displaying content in a rich format. Through these experiences I have developed useful skills, but look forward to enhancing my knowledge as I work with experienced colleagues and continue to educate myself. I learn quickly and thrive on collaborating with others to envision and develop new ideas and solutions.
