At the Utah Valley University Sutherland Archives, I cataloged and digitized hundreds of items ranging from college newspapers and yearbooks to personal manuscript collections and peace activist oral histories. I learned how to automate scanning processes and image editing with Photoshop. I also learned how to manage large amounts of image files as I amassed more collections during my work at UVU.

The experiences I had at UVU's Archives taught me the value of organization, synthesis, and quality assurance. Working with the staff archivist, I completed my tasks in good time and produced exceptional products that were influential in spreading the word about the Archives' new location and facilities in the Utah Valley University Library.
