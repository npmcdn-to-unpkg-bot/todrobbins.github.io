Book of Abraham Project

What I Did

  • Designed a new user interface for a collection of religious texts
  • Implemented a basic MySQL database

What's Next

  • Learn how to extract name entities for text retrieval (people, places, and things)
  • Learn how to encode/display the texts with LaTeX

This project has been a collaboration with a Brigham Young University professor over the past few years. He has assembled a large array of resources concerning the life of the Mormon prophet Joseph Smith, Jr., and his successors. I have been assisting with information architecture and designing a new site for him in my free time. I have utilized technologies such as: HTML5, CSS, Javascript, and MySQL/PHP for text retrieval. We also run informal user tests to ensure the user experience is acceptable to casual users and researchers alike.

I have learned many useful skills in the process, including processing large text data sets and manipulating those texts for display on the Web. Our collaboration is ongoing and we intend to release a new version of the site in the coming months.

Humless

What I Did

  • Built an e-commerce site with HTML, CSS, and Javascript

What's Next

  • Refine my web design skills by learning and applying responsive web design principles

I was hired as a freelance contractor by Humless to design their initial web presence as a new manufacturing company. I developed the site using new techniques in HTML5 and CSS3, focusing on user experience. I helped the company find its brand by focusing on content that mattered.

Working with Humless through the various iterations of the design mockups, production prototypes, and final website was a much needed growth experience in my professional development as a user experience designer. The opportunity to work as a freelance designer allowed me to interact and collaborate with Humless on important design decisions.

Utah Forest Stewards

What I Did

  • Customized a WordPress installation for a non-profit
  • Coordinated community outreach as the organization launched

What's Next

Working with the founder of Utah Forest Stewards, I set up and developed the WordPress site for their organization. I administer the site and contribute content occasionally. The Utah Forest Stewards site is one of many WordPress sites I have created, designed, or administered in the past 5 years.
